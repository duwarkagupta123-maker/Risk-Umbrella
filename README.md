# ☔ Risk Umbrella

**One umbrella for your stuff and your people.**

Risk Umbrella is an interactive insurance gap analyzer built for the **Finvasia Innovation Hackathon 2026**. It helps Indian consumers understand what’s covered and – more importantly – what’s **NOT covered** in their home, car, and life insurance policies. Enter your PIN code, paste your policy text, simulate real‑world disasters, and see your financial exposure in plain language.

---

## ✨ Key Features

| Feature | What it does |
|---------|---------------|
| **Dashboard** | Policy decoder (translates legal jargon into simple bullet points), visual umbrella that shows coverage gaps, real‑time health score, monthly premium tracker |
| **Policies** | View all your policies (home, car, life) in one place. Click any policy to see a **plain‑language translation** of what’s covered and what’s excluded (e.g., “Flood damage ❌ NOT covered”) |
| **Risk Simulator** | Enter your home value, car value, annual income, and number of dependents. Simulate a combined disaster (e.g., house fire + disability) and see the out‑of‑pocket cost and impact on your child’s college fund |
| **Claims Center** | Active claims tracking, claim history, and a step‑by‑step wizard to file a new claim |
| **Login** | Mock authentication (any email/password works – demo only) |
